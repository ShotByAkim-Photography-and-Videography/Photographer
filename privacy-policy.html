Privacy Policy

Your privacy and digital asset security are paramount. This policy documents how ShotByAkim Photography and Videography handles data.

1. Introduction

ShotByAkim Photography and Videography ("we", "our", or "us") operates the website located at https://shotbyakimpav.wixsite.com/photographer and provides premium digital media production services in Boiling Springs, SC 29316. We are dedicated to protecting the confidentiality and privacy of the personal information and digital creative assets shared with us by our clients.

2. Information We Collect

To deliver our luxury photography and videography services, we collect various types of user information, including:

  • Identifiable Contact Details: Name, phone number, email address, physical billing addresses, and wedding/event locations.
  • Creative Assets: High-definition photographs, raw video data, client voice overs, event schedules, and aesthetic mood boards.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser specifications, and usage behaviors collected automatically via analytic cookies on our website.

3. How We Use Collected Data

All data collected from clients is utilized exclusively for the delivery of premium, high-performance visual solutions:

  • Scheduling, planning, and executing sessions and commercial media projects in Boiling Springs, SC.
  • Post-production editing, color grading, asset proofing, and delivering completed media packages.
  • Managing communications, client contracts, digital billing receipts, and client satisfaction surveys.
  • Optimizing website functionality and conversion performance under professional security parameters.

4. Digital Asset Security & Archiving

We deploy high-end, multi-stage physical and digital cloud systems to safeguard client photographs and cinematic video reels. High-definition final master files are stored in a secure cloud-native vault accessible only via unique private invite tokens. We retain master files for a limited period under standard redundancy rules, but urge clients to maintain duplicate local backups.

5. Information Sharing & Third-Party Actions

We absolutely do not sell, rent, or lease private client contact information or creative assets to third parties. We may disclose technical information only to highly secure cloud-hosting and payment gateway processing units (such as Wix or Stripe) to complete necessary customer transactions.

6. Client Rights & Choice

Clients maintain comprehensive rights over their data. You can request changes, updates, or complete permanent deletion of your private files, billing logs, and online portfolio proofs at any time. Simply submit a written request directly to akimalston.aa@gmail.com or call us at (843) 476-5700.

7. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify or expand this Privacy Policy as local laws or security standards evolve. Significant changes will be posted clearly on this page with an updated modification date.
